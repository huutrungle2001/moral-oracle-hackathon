# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Moral Oracle

## 1. Introduction
**Moral Oracle** is a "wisdom staking game" where intelligence is valued over loud opinion. Users stake on the collective reasoning of the crowd and the logic of AI, turning moral debates into a rewarding intellectual playground.

### 1.1 Slogan
"Play to Think – Think to Earn"

### 1.2 Mission
To help people learn to think like an intelligent mind, reason like a philosopher, and act like a responsible individual. Intelligence is for creating real value.

## 2. Core Positioning
*   **Not just a debate platform**: A place where sharp thinking is rewarded.
*   **Wisdom Staking**: Users stake on reasoning, not just popularity.
*   **The Moral Shock**: The realization when collective emotional voting contradicts AI's objective reasoning, driving curiosity and learning.

## 3. Core Features & Phases

### Phase 1: Discover
*   **Homepage**: Trending duels with intriguing titles.
*   **Search/Filter**: By category or "Moral Intensity".

### Phase 2: Create & Curate
*   **Sources**: User-created or System-generated.
*   **AI Moderation**: Filters hate speech, personal attacks, and illegal topics.
*   **Requirements**: Short title, Context (4-6 lines), Two Outcomes (YES/NO).

### Phase 3: Vote & Debate
*   **Action**: Vote YES/NO + Write Argument (Max 300 chars, no replies).
*   **Staking**: Users stake tokens to validate their confidence.
*   **Upvoting**: Users curate the best arguments (affects rewards).
*   **Real-time Stats**: YES/NO ratio, Countdown timer.

### Phase 4: AI Verdict
*   **Adjudicator**: The AI Agent acts as the final judge.
*   **Criteria**:
    1.  **Logical Validity**
    2.  **Human-centered Reasoning**
    3.  **Long-term Consequences**
*   **Output**: Winning side declared + "Best Arguments" showcased (Most Logical, Most Humane, Most Creative).

### Phase 5: Personalized Feedback
*   **The Oracle Speaks**: The AI sends private feedback to participants highlighting strengths and weaknesses in their reasoning.

### Phase 6: Profile & Reputation
*   **badging**:
    *   *The Sage*: 5 consecutive wins.
    *   *Master of Reasoning*: Top arguments in 3 cases.
    *   *Trendsetter*: Created viral scenarios.
*   **Scores**: Win Rate, Total Upvotes, "Moral IQ".

## 4. Reward Mechanism (Smart Contract)
**Goal**: Every intellectual contribution has value.

| Category | Allocation | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Winning Side** | **68%** | Shared equally among correct voters. |
| **Top Args (Win)** | **15%** | Distributed to top 10 arguments on winning side (weighted by upvotes). |
| **Top Args (Loss)**| **15%** | Distributed to top 10 arguments on *losing* side. (Reward for good reasoning even if wrong verdict). |
| **Platform Fee** | **2%** | Protocol treasury. |
| **Creator Bonus**| **1%** | Taken from Platform Fee if scenario > 100 participants. |

## 5. Vision & Roadmap
*   **MVP**: Independent web app with core gameplay.
*   **Expansion**: Mini-apps (Zalo, Reddit Plugin, Discord Bot).
*   **Data Monetization**: "Moral Oracle for Enterprise" – Ethical training and bias analysis.
